# Primary Sources Used

1. **Bowring, John.** *The Kingdom and People of Siam, Vol. 1* (1857).  
   URL: https://archive.org/download/kingdompeoplesi1bowr/kingdompeoplesi1bowr.pdf

2. **British Foreign Office / Consul-General in Siam.** *Report by Her Majesty's Agent and Consul-General in Siam for the Year 1880* (published 1881).  
   URL: https://seadelt.net/Asset/Source/Document_ID-267_No-01.pdf

3. **Siamese Section, Louisiana Purchase Exposition (ed. A. Cecil Carter).** *The Kingdom of Siam* (1904).  
   URL: https://ia601301.us.archive.org/22/items/kingdomofsiammin00louirich/kingdomofsiammin00louirich.pdf

4. **Office of the Historian, U.S. Department of State.** *Law for the Abolition of Slavery, Ratanakosindr Sok 124* (1905 document reproduction).  
   URL: https://history.state.gov/historicaldocuments/frus1905/d930

5. **Office of the Historian, U.S. Department of State.** *Siam-French Convention and related diplomatic correspondence* (1905 document reproduction).  
   URL: https://history.state.gov/historicaldocuments/frus1905/d927

6. **British Treaty Series.** *Declaration between the United Kingdom and France concerning Siam, Madagascar, and the New Hebrides* (signed 1904; printed 1905).  
   URL: https://treaties.fcdo.gov.uk/data/Library2/pdf/1905-TS0007.pdf
